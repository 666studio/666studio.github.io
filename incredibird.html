IncrediBird

Have you ever dreamed of flying? Enjoy this casual and fun game where you can fly like a bird. The goal of the game is to fly through as many gates as possible and collect gold coins. You can challenge your friends with your incredible "fly-ability". Beyond all that, it can be a great full-body exercise if you choose the active modes! You can fly with four movements: flapping, squat, arm-opening, and arm-pushing. And you can fly towards wherever your imagination leads you!
